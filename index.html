MoneyVision Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

MoneyVision is an educational and travel-focused currency reference application. This Privacy Policy explains how MoneyVision handles information when you use the app.

1. Purpose of the App

MoneyVision helps users identify supported banknotes using their phone camera and OCR-based recognition. The app may display educational reference information about currencies, banknotes, denominations, materials, security features and official source links where available.

2. Camera and Image Use

MoneyVision may request access to your device camera so you can capture a banknote image for recognition. Captured images are used for banknote recognition and educational reference purposes.

MoneyVision does not use captured images for advertising, social sharing or unrelated media access. The app is designed to process images only for the functionality requested by the user.

3. Banknote Recognition

MoneyVision uses OCR-based recognition and reference data to identify supported banknotes. Recognition results are provided for educational and informational purposes only.

MoneyVision does not authenticate banknotes, does not certify whether a banknote is genuine or counterfeit, and must not be used as an official verification or anti-counterfeit tool.

4. Exchange Rate Data

MoneyVision may display currency exchange rates for educational and travel reference purposes. Exchange rate information may be provided through third-party exchange rate data services and/or official public reference sources, such as central bank reference rates where available.

Exchange rates shown in the app are informational only, may be delayed, and may differ from rates used by banks, card providers, exchange offices, payment processors or other financial institutions.

MoneyVision does not provide financial advice, does not guarantee exchange rate accuracy, availability or suitability for transactions, and should not be used as the sole source for financial decisions.

5. Account and Authentication

MoneyVision may allow users to sign in using supported authentication methods such as email/password or Google sign-in. Authentication is used to manage access to app features and, where applicable, premium access.

6. Premium Features and Payments

If MoneyVision offers premium features, purchases are handled through Google Play Billing. MoneyVision does not directly collect or store your payment card details.

7. Data We May Process

Depending on the features used, MoneyVision may process:

  • Camera images captured by the user for banknote recognition;
  • Recognition results such as country, currency, denomination and serial text when detected;
  • Account identifiers if the user signs in;
  • Purchase status if premium features are used;
  • Basic app usage or diagnostic information needed to operate and improve the app.

8. Data Sharing

MoneyVision does not sell personal data. Some app features may rely on trusted third-party services, such as authentication providers, exchange rate data providers, cloud hosting, database services or Google Play services.

9. Data Retention

MoneyVision keeps data only as long as necessary to provide app functionality, maintain user access, comply with legal obligations or improve app reliability.

10. Children

MoneyVision is not specifically directed to children. The app is intended for general educational and travel reference use.

11. User Choices

You can deny camera permission, but banknote scanning may not work without camera access. You may also manage app permissions through your device settings.
